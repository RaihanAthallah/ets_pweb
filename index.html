Books Categories

There are many book categories such as

Textbooks

A textbook is a book containing a comprehensive compilation of content in a branch of study with the intention of explaining it

Science

A science book is a work of nonfiction, usually written by a scientist, researcher, or professor like Stephen Hawking (A Brief History of Time), or sometimes by a non-scientist such as Bill Bryson (A Short History of Nearly Everything)

History

A book that describes (and explains) the history of a country, area or subject

Biography

A biography is simply the story of a real person's life. It could be about a person who is still alive, someone who lived centuries ago, someone who is globally famous, an unsung hero forgotten by history, or even a unique group of people

Adventure

Adventure fiction is a type of fiction that usually presents danger, or gives the reader a sense of excitement

Fantasy

Fantasy is a genre of speculative fiction involving magical elements, typically set in a fictional universe and sometimes inspired by mythology and folklore.
